So what the F#(% is a Swankie Yankee

Well, a Swankie Yankee, a term commonly used in the Deep South of the United States of America, is a term used for a person who has moved directly or indirectly from their place of birth to Seattle Washington before ultimately moving to Detroit Michigan.

Although a Swankie Yankee is a person who lived in Seattle for no more than three years and no less than 18 months, the term is used by some for any person moving from Seattle to Detroit.

Although it has been used erroneously, a Swankie Yankee can not be a person who moved to Detroit from Seattle indirectly: for example, a person who has lived in Seattle for 2 years before then moving to Cleveland Ohio for 1 year and then ultimately moving to Detroit can not be a Swankie Yankee because of their extended residency between living in Seattle and Detroit.
